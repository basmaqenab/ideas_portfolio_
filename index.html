ArtisanAl – AI-Powered Furniture & Art Visualization

The Problem

Customers struggle to visualize furniture in their homes, lack access to traditional artisans, and face overwhelming choices without guidance. Meanwhile, artisans struggle to find modern clientele and designers lack exposure.

The Solution

An intelligent platform that uses AI to analyze room photos, auto-fill with furniture recommendations, and connect users with artisans, designers, and manufacturers through a unified marketplace.

Key Features

  • AI Room Analysis: Detects space dimensions, layout, colors, lighting, and style from uploaded photos.
  • Auto-Visualization: Automatically fills rooms with suitable furniture and art in 2D & 3D.
  • Smart Recommendations: AI recommends items based on style, budget, room function, and preferences.
  • Alternative Options: Toggle colors, materials, price ranges, and artisan vs industrial options.
  • Custom Requests: Request bespoke designs from designers and craftsmen.
  • Unified Marketplace: Onboard artisans, designers, manufacturers, and galleries.
  • Order & Deliver: Visualize, order, and receive furniture delivered right to your doorstep.

Existing Competitors

Houzz

  • Home design platform with product marketplace and visualization tools
  • Limitations: Limited Al capabilities, no artisan focus, primarily Western-centrice designs

IKEA Place

  • AR app for visualizing IKEA furniture in your space
  • Limitations: Only IKEA products, no marketplace for other sellers, no custom design optioins

Modsy

  • Online interior design service with 3D room visualization
  • Limitations: Expensive subscription model, no artisan marketplace, limited to US market

Wayfair

  • Large e-commerce furniture platform with AR features
  • Limitations: No Al-powered room analysis, no artisan support, overwhelming product selection without personalization

Business Model & Tech

Revenue Model

  • 10-20% commission on sales
  • Subscription plans for creators
  • Custom design fees, AI visualization credits
  • Advertising

Tech Stack

  • Room segmentation AI, Object detection
  • 2D & 3D generation
  • Web platform, Mobile apps (iOS/Android)

Target Market

  • Homeowners & renters
  • Interior design enthusiasts
  • Traditional artisans, Furniture manufacturers
  • Art galleries

Our Competitive Advantages

  • AI-powered automatic room analysis and furniture placement – no competitors offer this level of automation
  • Unified marketplace connecting artisans, designers, and manufacturers in one platform
  • Focus on Middle Eastern traditional arts and craftsmanship – an underserved market
  • Both 2D and 3D visualization from a single photo upload
  • Custom design requests directly to craftsmen – bridging traditional skills with modern commerce
  • Price range filtering across artisan and industrial options for all budgets
  • No existing platform combines AI visualization with a marketplace that supports traditional artisans in the Middle East

Unique Value Proposition

First unified ecosystem merging AI visualization + marketplace + artisan support, focusing on Middle Eastern traditional arts.

BookMap – AI-Powered Interactive Book Gallery App

The Problem

Book fair attendees struggle to find specific books among thousands of booths. Small publishers lack exposure, and visitors can't access books after events end.

The Solution

An app with AI-powered book search, interactive booth navigation, location-aware event mode, and year-round delivery options for all participating publishers.

3D Visualization

Video Demo

Key Features

  • Al Book Search: Search by title, author, genre, or keywords with personalized recommendations.
  • Interactive Map: Locate books by booth/shelf with step-by-step navigation a n d optional AR overlay.
  • Event Mode: Auto-switches to fair mode based on location with live offers and booth updates.
  • Delivery Service: National and international delivery with pre-order options.
  • Year-Round Access: Browse and purchase from participating publishers anytime.
  • Publisher Dashboard: Upload inventory, manage offers, and view analytics.

Existing Competitors

Goodreads

  • Social cataloging website for books with recommendations
  • Limitations: No book fair integration, no navigation features, no purchasing capability

Amazon Kindle

  • E-book marketplace with vast selection
  • Limitations: No physical book fair support, no indoor navigation, focuses on digital books

Book Fair Apps

  • Individual fair apps created by organizers
  • Limitations: Only work during events, no year-round access, poor search functionality, no delivery options

LibraryThing

  • Social cataloging and book database
  • Limitations: No marketplace, no fair integration, limited to cataloging only

Business Model & Tech

Revenue Model

  • Publisher participation fees
  • Featured promotions
  • Delivery fees, Premium subscriptions
  • Analytics reports

Tech Stack

  • Al recommendation engine, Indoor navigation
  • AR guidance
  • ISBN scanning, Cloud database

Target Market

  • Book enthusiasts
  • Students & researchers
  • Publishers large and small, Niche/underrepresented publishers
  • Literary tourists

Our Competitive Advantages

  • First platform to bridge physical book fairs with year-round digital access
  • Al-powered book search with personalized recommendations based on reading preferences
  • Indoor navigation with AR overlay to locate specific books and booths
  • Event mode that automatically activates based on location during fairs
  • Gives visibility to small and underrepresented publishers who struggle at traditional fairs
  • National and international delivery extends book fair reach globally
  • Book fairs remain largely offline experiences with no unified digital platform. Visitors struggle to find specific books, small publishers get overlooked, and once the fair ends, access to those publishers disappears. No platform currently solves all these problems together.

Unique Value Proposition

Transforms traditional book fairs into digital, interactive, globally accessible experiences while promoting underrepresented publishers.

Hybrid Tourism Platform – AI-Powered Physical & Virtual Experiences

The Problem

Travelers seek all-in-one booking but must use separate apps for guides, hotels, and transport. Virtual tourism is underutilized, and student workforce potential remains untapped.

The Solution

An AI platform that automatically bundles guides, drivers, translators, hotels, and virtual experiences based on budget, preferences, and schedule with integrated student workforce.

Key Features

  • Al Service Matching: Automatically selects and bundles all travel services based on preferences.
  • Virtual & VR Tours: Access museums, galleries, and sites online with interactive experiences.
  • Student Integration: Students act as translators and co-guides for flexible service delivery.
  • Itinerary Optimization: Al optimizes timing, costs, and hybrid experiences.
  • Ratings & Trust: Verified profiles, rating system, and Al fraud detection.
  • Low-Season Revenue: Virtual content and dynamic pricing for year-round engagement.

Existing Competitors

TripAdvisor

  • Travel platform for reviews, bookings, and recommendations
  • Limitations: No Al bundling, no virtual tours, separate bookings for each service required

Airbnb Experiences

  • Local experiences and tours marketplace
  • Limitations: Limited to physical experiences, no VR options, no integrated transport or guide bundling

GetYour Guide

  • Tours and activities booking platform
  • Limitations: No Al personalization, no student workforce, no virtual experiences

Google Arts & Culture

  • Virtual museum tours and cultural content
  • Limitations: No booking capabilities, no physical service integration, purely virtual content

Business Model & Tech

Revenue Model

  • Booking commissions
  • Virtual experience tickets
  • Al bundling premiums, Partnership revenue sharing
  • Advertising

Tech Stack

  • Al recommendation engine, VR/360° integration
  • Booking APIs
  • Payment processing, Partner dashboards

Target Market

  • International tourists
  • Remote travelers
  • Language students, Local guides & drivers
  • Hotels & museums

Our Competitive Advantages

  • Al automatically bundles guides, drivers, translators, and hotels into one optimized package
  • Hybrid model combining physical tourism with VR/virtual experiences for remote access
  • Student integration creates affordable workforce while providing real-world experience
  • Year-round revenue through virtual tours during low tourist seasons
  • Single platform replaces multiple apps for complete travel planning
  • Al fraud detection and verified profiles ensure trust and safety
  • Travelers currently use 5-7 different apps to plan trips. No platform offers Al-driven bundling of all services with virtual alternatives. The student workforce potential in tourism is completely untapped, and virtual tourism remains disconnected from physical travel planning.

Unique Value Proposition

The only platform combining AI personalization, hybrid tourism, student integration, and all-in-one service bundling.

  • Includes rare and mind sports (chess, esports, archery) not available on other platforms

  • Al recommends sports based on interests, physical abilities, and goals
  • Mini Polympics tournaments create engagement and sponsorship opportunities
  • First comprehensive sports marketplace targeting the Middle East market

Unique Value Proposition

Untapped market in the Middle East with AI-driven personalization, rare/mind sports, and Mini Polympics events.
